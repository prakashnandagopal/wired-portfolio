Featured Project

The British Music Experience

From Static Archive to Knowledge Graph: A lesson in interactive discovery.

The Challenge

The BME had a rich database of over 50 years of music history, but it was locked in static lists. Visitors couldn't "see" the influence of one artist on another. They needed a way to explore connections—not just search for names.

The Solution

I developed a Knowledge Graph powered by a Headless CMS (Strapi). Instead of traditional pagination, I built a semantic search interface where queries return "Nodes" (Artists) connected by "Edges" (Influence/Genre).

Why This Matters for WIRED

This mirrors the future of digital journalism. It's not just about the article; it's about the context. This project proves I can build the complex frontend logic required to visualize deep investigative datasets.
